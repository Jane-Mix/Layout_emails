About me

My name is Lizaveta the Beautiful – a professional photographer. Catching magic between two lovers is my gift! I'm lucky to make my dream come true and travel around the world, capturing the best days of your life and making friends around all over the world. I like weddings, I like to travel, and most of all I love weddings in different countries and unusual adventures. Meeting different people from different countries and cultures and celebrating together significant events – it's great! I love life and the moments when I get a sense of freedom and the opportunity to enjoy it.
