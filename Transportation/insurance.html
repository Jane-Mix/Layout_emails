WHAT IS IT?

Transport insurance is understood as a set of various types of insurance that create conditions for compensating for various kinds of financial losses arising from participants in the transport process as a result of circumstances beyond their control.

Transport insurance is divided into:

Comprehensive car insurance "Kasko"

Cargo insurance and vehicles "Cargo"

Carrier liability insurance for cargo to its owners and third parties

RELIABLE INSURANCE COMPANIES

Please note that the carrier's liability insurance in the event of an insured event does not cover all losses. This should be taken into account when transporting high-value goods. In this case, we always warn the client that the carrier's insured liability implies only partial compensation and for such cargo additional insurance of the cargo itself is required. The client can insure his cargo both on his own and use our help.

The insurance contract can be concluded according to one of the options:

  • option (A) - "With responsibility for all risks";

  • option (B) - "With liability for loss and damage to goods";

  • option (C) - "Limited Liability for Loss and Damage to Goods".

The amount of the insurance rate is set by the Insurer, taking into account the terms of insurance, the characteristics of the goods, the route, the vehicle and the specifics of transportation, which are essential for determining the degree of risk.
